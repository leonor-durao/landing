Djerf Avenue has always been something I’ve wanted to build. It’s been a dream since I was a little girl. Djerf Avenue was created in 2019. The name came to us when we were having breakfast at All Time in Los Feliz, LA. We want you to feel like you’re walking down an avenue that breathes Djerf Avenue when you enter our world. Djerf Avenue is a Swedish, non-seasonal, ready-to-wear brand. Born out of our love for vintage and the perfect basics, Djerf Avenue is made to embody the confident, chic, and laid-back person on the go. We want to deliver pieces that feel timeless and that will last.
